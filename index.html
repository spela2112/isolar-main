Isolar Design

We are a boutique interior design agency providing modern and functional interior design for your home or business.

Our work

We are a team of interior design enthusiasts who love modern but functional design that turns spaces into homes or cosy and productive work spaces. We founded our agency in 2013 and since then we have transformed more than 100 homes and businesses, always following our vision to design for the people living or working there. While our individual aesthetic styles always inform our decisions, we love working with our clients and discovering their own sense of style and trying to reflect their personality and interests in the designs we provide.

About Us

We are a small agency with a personal, highly custom approach to design. You can read our stories below.

Spela's story

Spela is a serial entrepreneur with a passion for interior design and after two successful exits from her previous ventures she decided to turn one of her hobies into a successful business. Spela loves the minimalistic Scandinavian design but infuses is with the feeling of being in an artist's studio, blending the two styles harmoniously and creating an environment with an eclectic, artistic spirit.

Spela Cedilnik, CEO and Founder

Jane's story

Jane is an experienced designer with a special flair for the so called 'boho' style, combinging rich colours and textures with any kind of background, turning the space into a romantic, youthful style that is both modern and retro at the same time. She does wonders with small spaces which are notoriously hard to design in a way that looks uncluttered but is still functional and doesn't require people to get rid of half of their possessions in favour of style.

Jane Smith, Senior interior designer

Peter's story

Peter is our go-to designer for the modern, urban design with an industrial edge. He can combine concrete floors, leather and neon signs into a cosy cafe, a futuristic office or the coolest retro home you've ever seen. He sometimes even surprises himself with the unusual design choices he makes but the outcome is always spectacular.

Peter Davies, Senior interior designer
